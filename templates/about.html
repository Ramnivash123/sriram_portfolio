SRIRAM TK

Hello, I'm Sriram — a professional photographer with over five years of experience. I began my journey in wedding and event photography, and over time, my creative focus evolved toward fashion and commercial photography. To strengthen my skills, I completed a professional photography program at Light and Life Academy and assisted multiple photographers for around 1.5 years, gaining valuable industry experience.

I have a keen eye for detail and a strong visual sense, with a deep understanding of lighting, composition, and storytelling. Whether it’s fashion, product, or portrait photography, I approach every project with passion, precision, and intent to deliver visually compelling results.

Let’s work together to create images that tell your story.
